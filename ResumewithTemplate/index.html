About

Hey!, let me introduce myself.

I’m a student-athlete who basically grew up balancing kicks and clicks. I’ve been a Taekwondo player for over a decade, which means I have strong discipline, fast reflexes, and an automatic urge to bow slightly when saying thank you (old habits die hard). Sports taught me resilience, focus, and how to stay calm under pressure — whether that’s during a match or when my code suddenly stops working for absolutely no reason. Being an athlete shaped my mindset: show up, work hard, improve every day… and maybe stretch first, because injuries are real.

At the same time, I’m pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology, majoring in Web Technology, where I build my skills in creating digital solutions and web experiences. I’m also deeply interested in graphic design, because apparently kicking things wasn’t enough — now I also move pixels around for fun. I enjoy combining technical knowledge with creativity to design projects that are both functional and visually engaging. My goal is to grow into a tech professional who blends discipline, innovation, and design — someone who can fight bugs in code just as seriously as I fight in the ring.
